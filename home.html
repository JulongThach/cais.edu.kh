First Chapel

📅 August 08, 2025

Our first chapel of the school year is a special time to gather as a community in worship and reflection. Students, teachers, and staff come together to seek God’s guidance, give thanks, and dedicate the new academic journey to Him. It sets a spiritual tone for the year, reminding everyone of our shared faith and purpose at CAIS.

School Start

📅 August 04, 2025

The first day of school marks the beginning of a new chapter filled with excitement, friendships, and opportunities. Students are welcomed with warm smiles and guided into a supportive, Christ-centered environment. We are thrilled to start this journey together toward academic and spiritual excellence.
